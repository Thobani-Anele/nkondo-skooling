Frequently Asked Questions

What is Nkondo Schooling?

Nkondo Schooling is an online platform where teachers and learners can engage in live classes, study together, and socialize in a fun and educational environment.

Can learners study without a teacher?

Yes! Learners can create their own study rooms, invite friends, or meet new people to learn together anytime.

What makes Nkondo Schooling special?

It combines the best of Zoom, Teams, and social media with a friendly AI teacher bot to guide learners and answer questions in multiple South African languages.
